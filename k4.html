GUIDLINES FOR PRATHYAKSHA SEVA BOOKING PILIGRIMS:

Note: Pilgrims who book for Pratyaksha Seva should bring the printed copy of their receipt. 1.For all Sevas & Pujas, Male sevadars shall wear Pancha with Kanduva and females shall wear Saree with Blouse invariably. Other dresses will not be allowed
2.Only couple (Wife & Husband) or single person will be allowed to perform the Seva on one ticket.
3.Children below 10 years of age are also permitted to follow their parents.
4.Other kith and kin will not be allowed along with sevadars of any Seva ticket holders. Those kith and kin should take Rs.500/- ticket each to enter into the temple only for having Darshanam and not to perform the Seva
5.An ID proof on the name of the ticket holder must also be shown.
6.This ticket is non-transferable and it cannot be cancelled.
7.Mobiles are strictly prohibited in Temple Premises.
8.Only single person will be allowed to perform Single Abhishekam ticket on one ticket .
9.Devotees must report just 15 minutes prior the Seva timings at the ritual spot.

GUIDLINES FOR DARSHANAM BOOKING PILIGRIMS:

Note: Pilgrims who book for Darshanam should bring the printed copy of their receipt.
1.All the Pilgrims in group ticket have to report together.
2.At the time of entry, all pilgrims shall produce the same original Photo ID (Aadhar Card) used during booking.
3.The pilgrims shall wear Traditional Dress only. Male: Dhoti, Shirt / Kurtha, Pyjama. Female: Saree / Half Saree / Chudidar with Dupatta.
4.Reporting and late reporting of Darshanam (after 1 1/2 hour only on booked slot).
5.The pilgrims should not carry any luggage/cell phones/electronic gadgets while reporting.
6.All bookings are FINAL: Postponement/advancement/cancellation/refund is not allowed.
7.Entry with foot wear is not permitted into the queue lines, mada streets and Temple.
8.SSVVSD reserves the right of cancellation of Darshan under Special Circumstances.
