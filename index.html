Tirumala is a spiritual place in Tirupati, Andhra Pradesh, where the famous Tirumala Venkateswara Temple is located, attracting millions of devotees. SPIRITUAL REMINDERS

Daily inspiration for your soul.

Medaram Jathara
Feb 21 - Feb 24

Medaram Jathara is a tribal festival that honors Goddesses Sammakka and Saralamma, who sacrificed their lives for their people. It is held every two years in Medaram village, Telangana. In 2024, the festival will be from February 21 to 24. Millions of devotees visit the festival to offer their prayers and offerings

Sankashtahara Chaturthi
Feb 28

Sankashtahara Chaturthi is a Hindu festival dedicated to Lord Ganesha, the remover of obstacles. It is observed on the fourth day of the waning moon in February 2024 i.e., February 28.Devotees fast, pray, and offer water to the moonrise. It is believed that Ganesha grants the wishes of his devotees on this day

Maha Shivarathri
March 8

Maha Shivaratri 2024 is a Hindu festival that honors Lord Shiva, the destroyer of evil and the transformer of life. It is on March 8, 2024. Devotees fast, chant, and offer prayers and milk to Shiva lingams throughout the night. It is believed that Shiva performed the cosmic dance of creation, preservation, and destruction on this night
